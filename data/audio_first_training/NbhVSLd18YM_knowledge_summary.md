## Core Concepts Taught

### 1. Change of Character (CHoCH)
**Definition:** Primary indication of trend reversal when market breaks the current strong swing point.

**Rules:**
- Bullish market: CHoCH = break below current strong swing HL with body close
- Bearish market: CHoCH = break above current strong swing LH with body close
- Must break STRONG swing points (validated with inducement)
- Timeframe-specific - each TF has independent CHoCH levels
- Requires confirmation through opposite swing formation

### 2. Strong Swing Point vs Weak Swing Point
**Strong Swing:** Validated swing that took inducement + body close beyond previous swing
**Weak Swing:** Swing without inducement OR liquidity sweep only

**Why It Matters:**
- Only strong swings can be used for CHoCH identification
- Retail traders mark ALL swings (weak + strong) causing false CHoCH signals
- SMC traders mark only strong swings avoiding Smart Money traps

**Detection:**
- Strong: Inducement taken ✓ + Body close beyond swing ✓
- Weak: Missing inducement ✗ OR wick-only breakout ✗

### 4. SMC Structure Mapping Process

**Step-by-Step Workflow:**
1. Start from most recent high
2. Find inducement (first valid pullback on left side)
3. Verify inducement is taken out
4. Check candle body closes below inducement
5. Check candle body closes above previous high
6. Mark as valid HH/BOS
7. Mark pullback as HL
8. Repeat for next swing

**Annotations Used:**
- HH = Higher High
- HL = Higher Low
- LL = Lower Low
- LH = Lower High
- IDM = Inducement
- BOS = Break of Structure
- X = Liquidity Sweep

---

### 5. Advanced Rules

**Inducement Shift:**
- When new high created without taking previous inducement
- Shift inducement from old level to new level (most recent pullback)
- Prevents false structure in impulsive moves

**Valid Pullback:**
- Must break the low of the highest candle in impulse
- Sideways consolidation without breaking = invalid pullback

**Impulse-Based Inducement:**
- When price moves as strong impulse without pullback
- Starting low/high acts as inducement

**Candle Close Validation:**
- ALL structural moves require body close beyond level
- Wick-only = liquidity sweep, not valid break
- Even 3-4 pip body close is sufficient

---

### 6. Liquidity Sweep vs CHoCH

**Liquidity Sweep:**
- Wick beyond swing BUT body closes inside
- Smart Money hunting stops
- Mark with 'X' annotation
- NOT a valid CHoCH

**CHoCH:**
- Body close beyond swing point
- Valid structural break
- Mark with CHoCH annotation
- Potential trend reversal

---

### 7. CHoCH Confirmation

CHoCH alone = potential reversal (not confirmed)

**Confirmation Requirements:**
- Bullish → Bearish: Need LL + LH after CHoCH
- Bearish → Bullish: Need HH + HL after CHoCH

**Trading Implication:** Trade reversal only after confirmation, not on CHoCH alone.

---

### 8. Timeframe-Specific Structure

Each timeframe has independent structure:
- **Daily/H4 CHoCH** = Major trend reversal
- **H1/M15 CHoCH** = Minor pullback within larger trend
- Must analyze each timeframe separately
- Multi-timeframe analysis requires identifying CHoCH on each TF independently
